ðŸš€ Getting Started

Course Cupid is an AI-powered course recommendation platform designed for University of Otago students. We help you discover papers that match your interests, career goals, and academic path. Think of us as your personal course matchmaker, using smart algorithms to suggest the perfect courses for you.

Getting started is easy! You can use Course Cupid in three ways:

  1. Take the Quiz: Answer questions about your interests and goals for personalized recommendations
  2. Chat with AI: Have a conversation with our chatbot to explore course options
  3. Browse Papers: Search and filter through all available University of Otago courses

No account required to browse, but creating one lets you save your preferences and track your favorite courses.

You can browse courses and get basic recommendations without an account. However, creating a free account gives you access to personalized features like saving quiz results, tracking favorite courses, getting tailored recommendations based on your profile, and accessing your chat history.

âœ¨ Using Course Cupid Features

The Course Cupid quiz is a series of questions designed to understand your interests, learning style, career goals, and academic preferences. Based on your answers, our AI algorithm generates personalized course recommendations.

The quiz typically takes 5-10 minutes to complete. You can retake it anytime to get updated recommendations as your interests evolve.

Our AI chatbot can help you with:

  • Finding courses related to specific topics or subjects
  • Understanding course prerequisites and requirements
  • Exploring career pathways and relevant courses
  • Comparing different papers
  • Getting suggestions for electives
  • Learning about course difficulty and workload

Just type your question naturally - the chatbot understands conversational language!

Visit the Papers page to browse all available University of Otago courses. You can search by course code, name, or filter by subject area, and more. Click on any course to see detailed information including description, prerequisites, and enrollment details.
