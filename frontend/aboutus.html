About Course Cupid

We're on a mission to help students find their perfect academic match through the power of AI and personalized guidance.

Our Story

Course Cupid was born from a simple observation: choosing the right courses can make or break a student's academic journey, yet most students navigate this crucial decision with little more than course catalogs and word-of-mouth recommendations.

We believed there had to be a better way. A way that combines the vast knowledge of educational data with the personal touch of understanding each student's unique goals, learning style, and aspirations.

That's when we created Course Cupid – an AI-powered platform that acts as your personal academic matchmaker, helping you discover courses that don't just fit your requirements, but truly align with your passions and career objectives.

Our Mission

To revolutionize course selection by providing intelligent, personalized recommendations that empower students to make informed decisions about their academic future and achieve their learning goals.

Our Vision

A world where every student finds their perfect academic path through intelligent technology, leading to more successful, fulfilling, and purpose-driven educational experiences.

What Makes Us Special

We combine cutting-edge AI technology with human insight to create a truly personalized course recommendation experience.

AI-Powered Intelligence

Our advanced algorithms analyze your academic history, interests, and goals to provide highly accurate course recommendations.

Personalized Matching

We don't just recommend courses – we find the perfect academic matches that align with your unique learning style and career aspirations.

Interactive Guidance

Chat with our AI counselor anytime to get instant answers, explore options, and refine your course selection strategy.
